Eco-Friendly Solutions
Sustainable Waste Management

Bi Turbo is a trusted recycling company specializing in the reuse, recycling, and recovery of valuable resources from electronic and industrial waste since 2008. We provide customized waste management solutions, including hazardous waste disposal and waste-to-energy services.

Our mission is to help businesses manage waste responsibly while minimizing environmental impact. With a team of skilled professionals and environmental experts, we deliver innovative, sustainable services. Join Bi Turbo in creating a cleaner, greener future through responsible waste management.

Questions

Have questions about e-waste recycling, industrial waste disposal, or how Bi Turbo Green helps businesses go green? Here are some of the most common inquiries from our clients — with clear, informative answers.

More About

What types of e-waste do you accept?

We accept a wide range of electronic waste including computers, laptops, mobile phones, circuit boards, servers, printers, and industrial electronic equipment. If it contains precious metals or recyclable components, we can process it sustainably.

Is your recycling process environmentally friendly?

Yes, our recycling operations are designed to meet strict environmental standards. We use certified technologies to minimize environmental impact, recover valuable resources, and ensure hazardous components are safely handled.

Do you offer customized waste management solutions?

Absolutely. We work closely with each client to assess their waste stream and develop a tailored solution — whether it’s regular collection, secure destruction, or resource recovery. Our goal is to align with your sustainability goals.

How can my business start recycling with Bi Turbo Green?

Getting started is easy. Just contact us through our website or call us directly. We'll schedule a consultation to understand your needs, then design a service plan that fits your waste management and compliance requirements.

Are your services certified and compliant?

Yes, Bi Turbo Green is certified under relevant environmental and industrial waste handling standards. We comply with national and international regulations to ensure legal and ethical recycling practices.
